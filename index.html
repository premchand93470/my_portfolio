About Me

A passionate business development professional with expertise in community building, brand partnerships, and content creation

Business Development Manager

As a Business Development Manager at Telugu Anime Guild, I've been instrumental in transforming a nascent community into a thriving ecosystem with 6K+ members in just 6 months. My role encompasses strategic partnerships, lead generation, event management, and team leadership.

I've successfully brought major brands like Crunchyroll and Comiccon India for promotional collaborations, and served as Outreach Media Partner for India Joy 2025 - Asia's Largest VFX Summit, establishing TAG as a significant player in the anime, gaming, and entertainment industry.

With over 2 years of experience as a Social Media Influencer and 3 years as a Content Creator, I bring a unique blend of creative and business acumen to drive growth and engagement.
